Help & Donate

We're on the verge of the biggest mass extinction since the dinosaurs because of human activity. But you can play a key role in saving many amazing species. Help us by donating now!

guarantee a future

By making a donation to the AFA, you're joining us in our promise to save wildlife and the environment. You'll get updates every month about how you can help guarantee a future for endangered animals.

Other Ways to give!

Monthly Giving

Help us save invaluable habitats. Protect elephants from ivory poachers. Make a difference today for animals as a Wildlife Protector with incredibly helpful monthly donations.

Volunteer

If you don’t have money to give, donate your time. Many organizations and zoos have volunteer programs that you can easily sign up for and participate in. You can help clean beaches, rescue wild animals or teach visitors all about how they too can save the environment.

Visit

Zoos, aquariums, national parks and wildlife refuges are all home to a great variety of wild animals. Learn more about our planet’s species from experts when you visit your local zoo or travel to others. See Earth’s most amazing creatures up close!

Speak Up

Share your passion for wildlife conservation with your family. Tell your friends how they can help. Ask everyone you know to pledge to do what they can to stop wildlife trafficking. Speaking up is crucial for raising awareness, help spread the word whenever you can!
